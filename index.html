Our Story

During our college years, we began volunteering our time in many different capacities in the local church. We had always felt fulfilled in volunteering, but never felt a burden to be missionaries. After college, we were married and continued volunteering at our church. We became involved in short-term mission trips while we served in Family Ministries with the youth group—we loved it! Once J.T. completed the ordination process, we were seeking God and asking Him how He would have us serve. God opened our eyes to the mission field and becoming full-time missionaries.

Mission

We have answered the call of the Lord to serve on the Texas/Mexico border. God has opened the doors for us to serve at Seminario Biblico Rio Grande as a support to the students, staff and families. Mobilizing this next generation of pastors and missionaries to share the gospel in countries where Latin Americans are more widely accepted, will allow God’s love to spread beyond the Rio Grande Valley and into the world..
